Welcome to Micah's Weather Analysis Webpage

This webpage contains a series of additional pages that are used to visualize weather data and analyses from 500 cities across the world. This data was collected using Python, the Open Weather API, and the Google Places API. Using what I deemed 'ideal weather' conditions, I found these cities to aid me in finding my next potential vacation spot. I then performed analyses on various weather conditions vs. Latitude to uncover relationships that latitude has with 'cloudiness %', 'humidity %', 'temperature (F)', and 'wind speed (MPH)'.

Using the navbar atop the page, you may navigate to different pages containing various parts of my analysis of ideal vacation spots, and their city's weather.

To find visual comparisons of each weather analysis side by side, you can click on the 'Visual Comparisons' item in the navigation bar.

To find an individual comparison with an in-depth analysis, you can click the desired comparison item from the 'Plots' dropdown menu in the navigation bar.

To find a responsive table containing the data used in the visualizations, select the 'Data' option from the navigation bar.

To return to this page, just click 'Home' or 'Micah's Weather Webpage' in the navigation menu.
